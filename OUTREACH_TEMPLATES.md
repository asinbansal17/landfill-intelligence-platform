## Key Points for All Communications

### What to Emphasize
✅ **Your age/status:** "First-year student" = less threatening, shows ambition
✅ **The problem:** Real crisis happening now (not theoretical)
✅ **Your approach:** Realistic (not just copying rich countries)
✅ **Your focus:** India-specific (not generic environmental talk)
✅ **Your goal:** Help organizations (not ego/grades)

### What to Avoid
❌ "This will solve climate change" (too grand)
❌ "India is doing everything wrong" (dismissive)
❌ "Copy Sweden exactly" (unrealistic)
❌ Asking for money (just ask for feedback)
❌ Spam (personalize each email)

### Tone
- Curious, not preachy
- Realistic, not idealistic
- Humble, not arrogant
- Action-oriented, not complaining

## SUCCESS TIPS

✅ **Be specific:** "I'm researching Delhi's landfill capacity" not "waste crisis"
✅ **Make it easy:** "2 quick questions" not "long phone call"
✅ **Give them a reason:** "Your insights help me build something useful"
✅ **Follow up once:** But not twice (respect their time)
✅ **Stay genuine:** This is a real project, real passion

Good luck! 🚀
