# 📄 OVERVIEW: 1-Page Project Summary

## The Problem
**India has 5-10 years before major landfill crisis.** Delhi's landfills will overflow in 2-3 years. India generates 377,000 tons of waste daily but only recycles 5% (vs Sweden's 99%). Cost of inaction: $120 billion/year in pollution and health.

---

## Your Solution
**Build an interactive web platform showing:**
- Why India's waste system is failing (root causes)
- How successful countries (Sweden, Japan, Germany, S. Korea) solved it
- What's already working in Indian cities (Pune, Surat, Chandigarh)
- Realistic policy recommendations adapted to India's budget & constraints

---

## Why This Works
- ✅ Shows the problem (urgent, real numbers)
- ✅ Shows global solutions (proven models)
- ✅ Shows local successes (inspiring, possible)
- ✅ Provides actionable recommendations (not just criticism)
- ✅ Tool for NGOs & government to use (advocacy weapon)

---

## Your Role
You don't need to code. You'll:
1. **Research** (Weeks 3-8): Gather data on waste systems globally + India cities
2. **Describe** (Weeks 10-16): Tell AI what pages should show, AI builds it
3. **Review** (Weeks 17-18): Test, polish, launch

---

## What You're Building

### 7 Pages:
1. **Homepage** - Map showing global waste crisis, India highlighted
2. **Global Comparison** - Table: Sweden 99% vs India 5%, investment gaps, timelines
3. **Country Deep-Dives** - Sweden/Japan/Germany/S.Korea: How they did it, why it works, lessons for India
4. **India's Situation** - Crisis overview: 3,100 landfills, root causes, impact
5. **India's Cities** - Ranking 15 cities: Tier 1 (Pune 45%), Tier 2 (Bangalore 25%), Tier 3 (Delhi 5%)
6. **Realistic Recommendations** - 3 phases over 15 years, cost-benefit analysis, jobs created
7. **Download & Share** - Export reports, email to NGOs, customize by state
